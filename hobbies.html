My Interests and Hobbies

Here are some of the things I love doing in my free time!

Tinkering with Computers

I have had a thing for modifying computer hardware ever since childhood.

The first time I touched a computer was when I was around 6 years old, using my mother's office computer.

Reading

I love reading, regardless if it's physical or digital, words or pictures, manga or comics. Reading has helped me through the pandemic when everything outside was locked down.

A series I've been eagerly following is a manga called "Juujika no Rokunin".

Coding

I have an interest in coding.

I am not particularly good at coding compared to my classmates though, so it's definitely a rough road.

Gaming

Much like any other young adult living int he 20th century, gaming has always been a part of me ever since childhood. I dabble in fps and horror games, but I excel in music games.

One game I cannot recommend enough is the game Nier: Automata.

Gardening

I have a small garden that I'm trying to sustain.

I started gardening when I saw my parents plant a calamansi tree, and thought to myself: what a good way to generate food.

Antique collecting

I have a hobby of collecting any antique items I come across, be it from ukay-ukay stores or thrift shops.

I started collecting antiques because I was fascinated about the quality of the item despite the obvious effects of time on the item.
